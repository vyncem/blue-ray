Brand and Corporate communications

Our expertise is in the development of communications strategies that build and position brands. We formulate strategies for transformational and event-driven branding challenges. We help shape the public discourse by ensuring that our partners pro-actively build the right relationships with the key influencers within the environment they seek to play. Our approach to helping brands manage their reputation and build on the elements that convey value: innovation, leadership, performance, commitment to community and philanthropy, sustainability and transparency. We unlock reputational value through a combination of traditional best practices in corporate and mass communication.

Place Branding

We craft and implement Nation, Regional and City Branding strategies for Countries, Counties and Cities. This is through intensive research to get an understanding of the place and its distinctiveness. Our role is to define the brand, to mould its image, to put it on the world mental map. We interrogate and then position the places’ capabilities and uniqueness in exports, investment, tourism and talent. We help discover the region’s character and figuring out how to narrate it back to the people who live there.We strive to create a place’s very identity that can guide a government in figuring out how to rise in the esteem of its neighbours, how to allocate its resources, how best to compose the face it presents to the world.
